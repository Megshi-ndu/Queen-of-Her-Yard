We are dedicated to empowering women entrepreneurs across Kenya by providing them with the tools, resources, and platform needed to grow their businesses and reach new markets.

Our mission is to create a supportive ecosystem where women can thrive as business owners, contributing to economic growth and community development.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about Queen of Her Yard

What is Queen of Her Yard?

Queen of Her Yard is a platform dedicated to empowering women entrepreneurs in Kenya by providing them with digital tools, resources, and a marketplace to showcase their businesses, connect with customers, and grow their enterprises.

How does Queen of Her Yard support women entrepreneurs?

We support women entrepreneurs through:

  • Free and premium business listings
  • Digital marketing and visibility
  • Business training and workshops
  • Community networking opportunities
  • Access to customers across Kenya
  • Revenue generation through our platform
