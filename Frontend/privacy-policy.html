Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 30, 2025

Your Privacy Matters to Us

This Privacy Policy describes how Queen of Her Yard ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you use our website, mobile applications, and services (collectively, the "Service").

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. By using our Service, you consent to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information You Provide:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, business name, and business type when you create an account
  • Business Details: Business description, location, contact information, services offered, and business photos
  • Communication Data: Messages, inquiries, and feedback you send to us
  • Payment Information: Billing address and payment details for premium services (processed securely through third-party payment processors)
  • Profile Information: Additional information you choose to include in your business profile

Information Automatically Collected:

  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, and device identifiers
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on pages, click-through rates, and navigation patterns
  • Location Data: General geographic location based on IP address (with your consent for precise location)
  • Cookies and Tracking: Information collected through cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

Service Provision:

  • Creating and managing your account
  • Displaying your business in our directory
  • Facilitating connections between business owners and customers
  • Processing payments for premium services
  • Providing customer support and responding to inquiries

Communication:

  • Sending service-related notifications and updates
  • Responding to your messages and inquiries
  • Sending newsletters and marketing communications (with your consent)
  • Notifying you about important changes to our services

Platform Improvement:

  • Analyzing usage patterns to improve our Service
  • Developing new features and services
  • Ensuring security and preventing fraud
  • Conducting research and analytics

Legal and Compliance:

  • Complying with legal obligations
  • Protecting our rights and interests
  • Enforcing our Terms of Service
  • Preventing misuse of our platform

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

Public Information:

  • Business name, description, and contact information are publicly visible in our directory
  • Business photos and content you upload may be displayed to other users
  • Any information you choose to make public through your profile

Service Providers:

  • Third-party service providers who assist with platform operations (hosting, payment processing, analytics)
  • Professional advisors (legal, accounting, business) when necessary
  • Marketing and advertising partners (only with your consent)

Legal Requirements:

  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety
  • To prevent fraud or security threats
  • In connection with a business transfer or acquisition

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on our platform:

Types of Cookies We Use:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for basic platform functionality
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings
  • Marketing Cookies: Used to deliver relevant advertisements (with your consent)

Managing Cookies:

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling certain cookies may limit platform functionality.

5. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information:

Security Measures:

  • Encryption of sensitive data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Access controls and authentication measures
  • Staff training on data protection and privacy
  • Secure data centers and hosting providers

Data Retention:

  • We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services
  • We may retain certain information for legal, accounting, or legitimate business purposes
  • You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time

International Transfers:

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than Kenya. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data during such transfers.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have several rights regarding your personal information:

Access and Portability:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Receive a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information

Deletion and Restriction:

  • Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal requirements)
  • Restrict processing of your personal information in certain circumstances
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time

Account Management:

  • Update your profile information through your account settings
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Control your privacy settings and communication preferences

Exercising Your Rights:

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@queenofheryard.com. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

7. Mobile Applications

If you use our mobile application, we may collect additional information:

Mobile-Specific Information:

  • Device information (model, operating system, unique device identifiers)
  • App usage data and performance metrics
  • Location information (with your permission)
  • Push notification preferences

App Permissions:

Our app may request certain permissions, including:

  • Camera access for business photos
  • Photo library access for uploading images
  • Location access for business verification
  • Notifications for important updates

8. Marketing Communications

We may send you marketing communications about our services and relevant business opportunities:

Communication Types:

  • Newsletter with business tips and platform updates
  • Promotional emails about premium features
  • Notifications about new businesses in your area
  • Event invitations and community updates

Opt-In and Opt-Out:

  • Marketing communications require your explicit consent
  • You can unsubscribe at any time using the unsubscribe link
  • You can update your communication preferences in your account settings
  • We will stop sending marketing communications if you opt out

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

10. Third-Party Links and Services

Our platform may contain links to third-party websites and services:

Third-Party Services:

  • Payment processors for premium subscriptions
  • Social media platforms for sharing and login
  • Analytics providers to understand platform usage
  • External business websites and contact forms

Privacy Practices:

  • We are not responsible for third-party privacy practices
  • We encourage you to read the privacy policies of any external services
  • We provide links to external services for convenience, not endorsement

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Sending you an email notification of significant changes
  • Displaying a prominent notice on our platform
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about our privacy practices.

12. Data Protection Compliance

We are committed to compliance with applicable data protection laws, including:

Legal Framework:

  • Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for EU users
  • Other applicable local and international privacy laws

Data Protection Officer:

We have appointed a Data Protection Officer who is responsible for overseeing our privacy practices. You can contact them at dpo@queenofheryard.com.

13. Data Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach that poses a risk to your rights and freedoms, we will:

  • Investigate the breach promptly
  • Notify the relevant data protection authorities within 72 hours
  • Contact affected users without undue delay
  • Provide information about the breach and steps taken
  • Recommend steps you can take to protect yourself
